What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that is secured by cryptography, making it nearly impossible to counterfeit. Unlike traditional currencies, it is decentralized, meaning it's not issued or regulated by any central authority. Instead, it operates on a technology called blockchain.

Popular Cryptos: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB), Solana (SOL), XRP, Cardano (ADA)

Use Cases:

  • Investment: Buy and hold crypto to gain value over time.
  • Payments: Use crypto to pay for services and products.
  • Staking: Lock your coins and earn passive income.
  • DeFi: Decentralized Finance platforms offer loans, savings, and trading without banks.
  • NFTs: Digital assets like art, music, and collectibles.
