What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography. It operates on blockchain technology and is decentralized — meaning no government or bank controls it.

Popular Cryptos: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB), Solana (SOL)

People use crypto for trading, investing, payments, and even earning passive income through DeFi and staking.

Crypto Tips

  • Never invest more than you can afford to lose.
  • Use two-factor authentication (2FA) on your accounts.
  • Keep your recovery phrases and private keys secure.
  • Start small and learn before going big.
  • Consider long-term holding (HODLing) over day trading.
  • Diversify across different coins, not just one.
  • Watch out for scams and "get-rich-quick" promises.
