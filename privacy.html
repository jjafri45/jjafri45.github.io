Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Overview

ToolDrop is a browser-based tools website. Many tools run entirely in the browser, but some pages may rely on third-party services for features such as translation, exchange-rate lookups, or QR rendering.

Information processed

ToolDrop does not require account registration for general use. However, like most websites, limited technical data may be processed through hosting, delivery, security, and advertising systems.

  • standard browser and device information
  • requested URLs and referrers
  • approximate usage and performance information
  • cookies or similar identifiers used by advertising or third-party services

Google AdSense and advertising

ToolDrop may use Google AdSense to display advertising. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads, personalize advertising, and limit repeated exposures.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You can manage ad personalization through Google Ads Settings. You can also learn more about interest-based advertising choices at optout.aboutads.info.

Third-party services

Some tools may call third-party APIs to provide live functionality. Examples include exchange-rate data, translation output, or QR rendering services. Requests sent to those services are governed by their own terms and privacy practices.

Cookies

ToolDrop may use cookies or similar technologies through advertising providers, analytics tooling if added later, or third-party services required to make certain tools work. Cookie behavior may change if site infrastructure or advertising providers change.

Data retention and control

Because ToolDrop is primarily a static site, the site owner does not maintain user accounts or a general-purpose user dashboard for stored personal data. For third-party advertising controls, use the opt-out and preference links referenced above.

Contact

For site questions, corrections, or policy issues, visit the Contact page.
